MOUCHETTE
ARCHIVE

A documentation and reconstruction effort around the early net art site mouchette.org, focusing on preservation, broken links, and participatory memory.

ARCHIVAL APPROACH

Working with the creator of mouchette.org to preserve and document the complex technical, social, cultural aspekts of the work. Recreating websites from traces, documenting instructions of use and documenting user participation.

NET ART PRESERVATION CONTEXT

Reconstruction and preservation of early web materials, including migration of obsolete formats, development of documentation guidelines, and reconstruction of historical website structures.

Our collaboration extends to projects such as I love mouchette and Visions of Mouchette which explore strategies for sustaining early web-based artworks within contemporary internet infrastructures.
