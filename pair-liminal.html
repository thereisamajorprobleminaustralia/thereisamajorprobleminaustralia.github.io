PAIR
PAVING
ACCESSIBLE REALITIES

P.A.I.R. (Paving Accessible Inclusive Realities) is a two-year European project (2024–2026) dedicated to redefining access and participation in culture.

It is implemented by liminal (Greece) in collaboration with Alternative Brains Rule (Cyprus) and the Estonian Agrenska Foundation (Estonia), with support from the European Union.

Mentorship of resident artist focusing on inclusive and accessible digital art practices and archiving.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Core Aim

To develop accessible artistic practices and tools that expand participation in contemporary culture for disabled and non-disabled artists alike.

Partners

liminal (Greece)
Alternative Brains Rule (Cyprus)
Estonian Agrenska Foundation (Estonia)

Focus

Accessibility
Digital media
Inclusive artistic production
European cultural collaboration

Description

P.A.I.R. supports experimentation with accessibility tools and digital media through artistic production, mentoring, and collaborative development. It positions accessibility as a creative and structural principle in culture.
